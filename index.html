Welcome to Blackjack

About the Game

Blackjack is a popular card game played in casinos around the world. The objective is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

How to Play

Players are dealt two cards and can choose to hit (take another card) or stand (keep their current hand). The dealer also has a hand, and players aim to have a higher total than the dealer without exceeding 21.

Game Rules

The game is played with one or more decks of standard playing cards. Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, face cards are worth 10 points, and all other cards are worth their face value.
